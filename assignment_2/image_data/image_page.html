3. Key Insights

  • ✓

    Key insight: Of all Machine learning algorithms, support vector machines perform the best, followed by logistic regression, random forest and voting classifier while Ada Boost performs the worst.

  • ✓

    Overall: although ensemble method seems to perform better, they also have longer training time than that of normal ML algorithms

  • ✓

    When adding dimension reduction into the feature processing pipeline: UMAP seems to have a negative effect on the accuracy and the training time, while PCA has a positive effect on slightly boosting accuracy and significantly shorten the training time.

  • ✓

    Regrading to pretrained model: they take significant longer time to train but have overall better result than previous method and more likely to increase if training with more epochs

  • ✓

    ViT-16/B: have the best accuracy both also have highest training time, similar to Restnet18

  • ✓

    Forest: seems to have the best accuracy while mountain and glacier have the least
